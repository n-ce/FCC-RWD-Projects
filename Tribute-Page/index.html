N I S A R G A D A T T A     M A H A R A J

born Maruti Shivrampant Kambli, was an Indian guru of nondualism.

  • Nisargadatta was born on 17 April 1897, in Bombay.
  • In 1933, he was introduced to his guru, Siddharameshwar Maharaj.
  • Siddharameshwar gave him instructions for self-enquiry which he followed.
  • In 1937, Maharaj left Mumbai and travelled across India.
  • In 1938, He returned to his family in Mumbai. On the journey home his state of mind changed.
  • In 1966, He retired but continued to receive and teach visitors in his home.
  • He died on 8 September 1981 at the age of 84, of throat cancer.


  • It is always the false that makes you suffer, the false desires and fears, the false values and ideas, the false relationships between people. Abandon the false and you are free of pain; truth makes happy, truth liberates.
